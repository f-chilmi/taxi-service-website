## 📋 Content Strategy

### Content Goals
- **Build Trust**: Professional, reliable, safety-focused messaging
- **Drive Action**: Clear calls-to-action for bookings and contact
- **Local Connection**: Turkish cultural sensitivity and local expertise
- **Transparency**: Clear pricing and service information
- **Accessibility**: Simple language accessible to diverse users

### Target Audience Content Needs

**Local Residents**
- Reliable daily transportation
- Competitive pricing
- Local route knowledge
- Professional service

**Tourists**
- English language support
- Airport transfers
- Sightseeing tours
- Cultural guidance

**Business Travelers**
- Professional service
- Punctuality emphasis
- Corporate rates
- Professional receipts

## 🌐 Bilingual Content Guidelines

### Language Priority

**Primary Language**: Turkish
- Native market focus
- Local cultural references
- Formal but warm tone

**Secondary Language**: English
- International tourists
- Business travelers
- Simple, clear language

### Translation Guidelines

**Tone & Style**
- **Turkish**: Warm, respectful, family-oriented
- **English**: Professional, clear, international

**Cultural Adaptation**
- Turkish version: Local landmarks, cultural references
- English version: Universal landmarks, international standards

**Technical Implementation**
```html
<!-- HTML Structure -->
<html lang="tr"> <!-- Default Turkish -->
  <div class="language-toggle">
    <button data-lang="tr" class="active">TR</button>
    <button data-lang="en">EN</button>
  </div>

  <h1 data-tr="Güvenilir Taksi Hizmeti"
      data-en="Reliable Taxi Service">
    Güvenilir Taksi Hizmeti
  </h1>
</html>
```

### Content Consistency Checklist

- [ ] All sections available in both languages
- [ ] Cultural references adapted appropriately
- [ ] Contact information consistent across languages
- [ ] Pricing in Turkish Lira for both versions
- [ ] Emergency information clear in both languages
- [ ] Navigation terms translated consistently
